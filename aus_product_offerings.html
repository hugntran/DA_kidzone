ABCya Games App

ABCya Games App

With the ABCya Games app, kids can practice everything from addition to animation with the help of dogs, magicians, and talking paintbrushes! Featuring over 300 educational games designed for ages 3 - 12, your students will love practicing math, language arts, and more on their phone or tablet with the ABCya Games app.

ABCya Bingo Collection App

ABCya Bingo Collection App

The ABCya BINGO Collection app features 11 bingo-style games with topics ranging from sight words to math facts to geography. Customize your game for any age or skill level by choosing a grid size and a specific area of focus within each topic. Kids will love unlocking new game pieces each time they get BINGO!

Create a Car App

Create a Car App

Design your dream car with ABCya’s Create a Car, a top-selling creativity app for children of all ages. First choose a body and customize your car with wheels, decals, parts, and accessories. Then watch your creation come to life as you collect power ups and avoid obstacles driving your car in the bonus mini game.

Donut Drop App

Donut Drop App

Do your kids like donuts, unicorns, and narwhals? Then they’ll love ABCya’s Donut Drop app! To play, move your character across the screen to collect the falling donuts, but be careful not to catch the wrong ones. Earn extra points by catching the donuts in the correct order.

Dreidel App

Dreidel App

Kids can celebrate the Jewish festival of Hanukkah from their phone or tablet with ABCya’s Dreidel App. Players will take turns spinning a dreidel to earn coins, or gelt. The game ends when one player has all of the gelt coins. The Dreidel App supports up to four players, so it’s sure to be a favorite at your holiday gathering!

Math Bingo App

Math Bingo App

ABCya’s Math Bingo app is a fun activity for kids to practice addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Adults can choose a grid size, difficulty level, and operations to customize the game for their student. To play, solve the math questions by tapping the correct answer on the bingo grid.

Word Bingo App

Word Bingo App

Children can practice sight word recognition with ABCya's Sight Word Bingo app. To play, tap the word on your board that matches the word read aloud by the game. Parents and teachers can select from one of seven word groups and choose a grid size to customize the game for any age or skill level.
